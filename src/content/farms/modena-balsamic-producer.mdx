Two products share the name and almost nothing else. *Aceto Balsamico Tradizionale* DOP is the artisanal, decades-aged thick black syrup; *Aceto Balsamico di Modena* IGP is the supermarket vinegar that's mostly wine vinegar coloured with caramel. Both are legal. Both are called *balsamic vinegar*. They are different products.
